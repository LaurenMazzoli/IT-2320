About Us

Internationals Soccer Club is based in Northeast Ohio in the Cleveland area. We are part of a select group of top clubs licensed to participate in the USSDA program and one of two USSDA Member Clubs in Ohio.

Players travel from areas near and far to participate in our DA program. Our alumni have achieved high levels of success throughout all levels of soccer including the MLS, European leagues, US MNT and top collegiate programs. 2016 marks the 10th year that Internationals has participated in the US Soccer Development Academy Program. We have a pay-to-play program. Scholarship applications are available.
